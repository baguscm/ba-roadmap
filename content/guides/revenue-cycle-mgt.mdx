# Revenue Cycle Mgt (RCM)

Mapped to "Manage Financial Resources" (APQC 9.0) and "Manage Performance" (APQC 12.0). RCM is the comprehensive "Front-to-Back" financial management of a patient's clinical journey.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Denial Management:** Analyzing why claims were rejected (e.g. "Service Not Covered") and orchestrating the "Appeal" process.
2. **Remittance Advice (EDI 835):** Processing the digital explanation of payment from the insurance company and reconciling it with the hospital ledger.
3. **Accounts Receivable (AR) Follow-up:** proactive outreach to payers for claims that have been "In-process" for more than 30 days.
4. **Patient Financial Engagement:** managing the final "Patient-pay" balance through payment plans, financing, or charitable care adjustments.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Financial Analysis:** calculating the "Net Collections Rate" (what was actually paid vs what the contract expected).
*   **Root Cause Analysis:** identifying the exact point in the intake/clinical/billing chain where a recurring "Denial" error is occurring.
*   **Process Analysis:** identifying manual "Work-arounds" in the billing department that can be replaced with RPA (Robotic Process Automation).

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> RCM is about "Predictability." As a BA, don't just solve one denial at a time. Use your data to predict which claims are "high risk" for denial BEFORE you even send them. Preventing a 45-day delay is worth 10x more than efficiently handling an appeal after the delay has happened.
