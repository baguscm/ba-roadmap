# Vendor Onboarding

In the **Procure to Pay (P2P)** cycle, the **Vendor Master** is the single source of truth for all external suppliers. **Vendor Onboarding** is the process of vetting, registering, and enabling a new partnership to ensure financial and legal compliance.

## Node Knowledge: The Supplier Lifecycle
1. **Qualification:** Verifying if the supplier meets quality and sustainability benchmarks.
2. **Data Collection:** Capturing W-9 forms (US), VAT/Tax certificates, and diversity status.
3. **Banking Verification:** Ensuring bank account details are authenticated to prevent fraud.
4. **Contract/NDA:** Anchoring the relationship with legal terms and conditions.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

*   **Risk Analysis:** Assessing the financial stability and reputational risk of a new vendor.
*   **Requirements Analysis:** Defining the "Mandatory Document" list for different vendor types (e.g., Service provider vs. Component manufacturer).

## Typical Deliverables

*   **Vendor Request Form:** The internal document used to initiate the onboarding of a new supplier.
*   **Master Data Definition:** Specifying which fields (Payment terms, Currency, Incoterms) are required for ERP setup.

## 💡 Expert BA Review

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Fraud lives in the Vendor Master. An unauthorized change to a bank account field can result in millions in losses. Your BA requirements must include "Segregation of Duties" (SoD) and "Dual Control" (Four-eyes principle) for any changes to sensitive vendor payment data.
