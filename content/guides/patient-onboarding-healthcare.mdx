# Patient Intake & Onboarding

A critical entry point mapped to "Manage Customer Service" (APQC 3.0) and "Execute Patient Logistics" (APQC 7.2) within the healthcare enterprise.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Intake & Demographics:** Capturing basic patient identity, contact information, and medical history via a secure portal.
2. **Insurance Discovery:** Verifying the primary/secondary insurance coverage and capturing ID card images.
3. **Consents & Disclosures:** Digitally executing HIPAA notifications, Clinical Consent forms, and Financial Responsibility agreements.
4. **Clinical Triage:** Collecting initial symptoms or reasons for the visit to prioritize appointment scheduling based on urgency.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Prototyping:** designing intuitive, accessibility-compliant web and mobile forms to reduce intake time.
*   **Interface Analysis:** mapping the data fields collected at intake directly to the core EMR/EHR system to prevent duplicate entry.
*   **Business Rules Analysis:** defining the "Mandatory" vs "Optional" fields required for first-time vs returning patients.

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Minimize "Wait-time friction." As a BA, your goal is to have 90% of the data collected BEFORE the patient reaches the physical clinic. If they have to fill out paper forms on a clipboard, you've missed an opportunity for digital transformation.
