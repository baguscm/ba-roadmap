# TMS & Carrier Management

Directly supports "Manage Outbound Transportation" (APQC 6.2) and "Select and Manage Carriers" (APQC 6.2.2). This node targets the technical integration of transportation systems.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **API Integration:** Connecting the ERP/WMS to carrier platforms (UPS, FedEx, DHL, Maersk) to retrieve real-time rates and labels.
2. **Load Tendering:** Automated offering of shipments to carriers based on pre-defined regional and price priority rules.
3. **Execution & Tracking:** Capturing real-time transit status via EDI 214 (Transportation Carrier Shipment Status) messages.
4. **Spot Market Biding:** managing "Mini-auctions" for urgent or specialized loads that fall outside of long-term carrier contracts.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Interface Analysis:** mapping thousands of disparate carrier error-codes into a unified user-friendly "System Message" (e.g. "Address Invalid").
*   **Data Modelling:** designing the "Rate Table" architecture that can handle seasonal surcharges and fuel adjustments.
*   **Non-Functional Requirements:** defining the millisecond responsiveness required for "Real-time Rating" during website checkout.

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Don't over-integrate. As a BA, don't build 50 direct carrier APIs. Implement a "Shipping Aggregator" (e.g. ShipStation, ShippyPro) that gives you 100 carriers through a single API connection. It reduces your technical debt by 90%.
