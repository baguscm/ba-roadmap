# Clearing & Settlement

Covers the "Manage Cash and Treasury Systems" and "Interbank Reconciliation" category.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Clearing:** Financial institutions exchanging payment instructions to calculate net positions.
2. **Netting:** Determining the final lump sum to be moved between banks at the day's cut-off.
3. **Settlement:** The actual movement of central bank funds (Fiat) to fulfill interbank obligations.
4. **Reconciliation:** Matching the final bank statement against internal platform clearing logs.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Process Modeling:** Mapping the T+1 or T+2 settlement timelines for different payment rails.
*   **Data Analysis:** Monitoring real-time liquidity to ensure all outgoing settlement obligations are funded.
*   **Root Cause Analysis:** Investigating "Batch Failures" or "Returns" in high-volume ACH/SEPA files.
