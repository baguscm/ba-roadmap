# Financial Reporting

Aligned with "Manage Financial Resources" (APQC 9.0) in the global e-commerce framework.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Journal Entry Generation:** Automatic the creation of debits and credits for every transaction.
2. **Sales Tax Reporting:** Calculating nexus and filing accurately with services like Avalara.
3. **P&L Breakdown:** Viewing revenue minus COGS (Cost of Goods Sold).
4. **Audit Trail:** Maintaining a history of every modification to a financial record.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Data Analysis:** Tracking Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) performance against targets.
*   **Business Rules Analysis:** Defining "Revenue Recognition" rules (Point-of-Sale vs. Point-of-Shipment).
*   **Regulatory Analysis:** Ensuring compliance with regional IFRS or GAAP accounting standards.

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Nexus is a moving target. As a BA, don't try to build tax logic yourself. Implement an integration to Avalara or TaxJar to handle the varying state-by-state laws automatically.
