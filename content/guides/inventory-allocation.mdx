# Inventory Allocation

Part of "Plan and Manage Inventory" in the APQC framework, specifically focusing on the logic of stock reservation.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Global Inventory Visibility:** Maintaining a real-time view of stock across all warehouses and stores.
2. **Safety Stock Buffer:** Reserving items to prevent over-selling (e.g., hiding the last 2 units from the website).
3. **Channel Allocation:** Reserving specific stock pools for Amazon vs. Shopify vs. Physical Retail.
4. **Pre-order Management:** Allocating inventory that is "In-Transit" but not yet physically received.

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Avoid "Overselling" at all costs. Over-selling 10 units on eBay might get your store banned. As a BA, always implement a "Safety Buffer"—even if you have 10 items, only tell the marketplace you have 8.
