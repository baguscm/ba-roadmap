# KPIs & Dashboards

A core part of "Manage Performance and Continuous Improvement" (APQC 12.5) for senior leadership.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Metric Definition:** Defining GMV (Gross Merchandise Value) vs. Net Sales vs. Shipped Sales.
2. **Visualization Design:** Choosing between Bar charts (trends), Gauges (targets), or Heatmaps (regions).
3. **User Profiling:** Designing tailored views for Executives (Strategic) and Warehouse Managers (Operational).
4. **Reporting Cycles:** Scheduling daily, weekly, and monthly automated email/Slack pushes.

---

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Prototyping:** Building low-fidelity wireframes of the dashboard layout for user feedback.
*   **Surveys/Interviews:** Eliciting the "North Star" metrics that drive operational behavior.
*   **Concept Modelling:** Defining the relationships between different metrics (e.g., how Traffic impacts CRO).

## Typical Deliverables

*   **Dashboard Wireframes / Mockups**
*   **KPI Definition Matrix (Calculations)**
*   **Drill-down Capability Specification**

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Avoid "Dashboard Exhaustion." Don't give an executive 50 metrics. Your job as a BA is to curate the board down to the "Magic three" that truly drive growth: Conversion, Margin, and Retention.
