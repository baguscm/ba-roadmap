# Payment Gateways & Rails

In **Advanced B2C**, **Payment Gateways** are the digital cashier of the **O2C** cycle. They provide the infrastructure to securely authorize, capture, and settle digital payments across various channels (Web, Mobile, App) while ensuring data security via tokenization.

## Node Knowledge: The Payment Lifecycle
1. **Authorization:** Temporarily booking the funds on the customer's card at the time of order.
2. **Capture:** Instructing the bank to actually transfer the authorized funds once the order is shipped.
3. **Refund/RMA:** Processing credit back to the original payment method for returned goods.
4. **Fraud Screening:** Using real-time AI to assess the risk of a transaction before authorization.

## 💡 Expert BA Review

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Minimize "Payment Latency". If your gateway takes too long to respond during a flash sale, the customer will abandon the cart. As a BA, you must define the non-functional requirements for API response times and ensure your system can handle "Asynchronous Capture" for high-volume transactions.
