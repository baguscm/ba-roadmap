# Asset Maintenance (EAM)

Associated with "Manage Physical Assets" (APQC 10.0) and "Manage Maintenance" (APQC 6.5) in the Energy sector. Focuses on the lifecycle of critical infrastructure.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Asset Onboarding:** Capturing the "Birth Certificate" of a new asset (e.g. Transformer, Turbine) including warranty and technical specs.
2. **Maintenance Scheduling:** using "Time-based," "Usage-based," or "Predictive" triggers to generate work orders.
3. **Field Service Execution:** Orchestrating crews, vehicles, and specialized tools to the physical asset location.
4. **Reliability Analysis:** Analyzing the "Mean Time Between Failure" (MTBF) and Root Cause codes to optimize future maintenance strategies.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Process Modeling:** mapping the "Emergency Breakdown" workflow to ensure safety-clearances (LOTO) are documented.
*   **Non-Functional Requirements:** defining the ruggedness and offline data-sync capabilities for field technician tablets.
*   **Solution Evaluation:** calculating the ROI of "Predictive Sensors" vs. the cost of a catastrophic asset failure.
