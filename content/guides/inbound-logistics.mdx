# Inbound Logistics

Mapped to the "Procure Materials and Services" (APQC 4.0) and "Manage Inbound Logistics" (APQC 6.2.1) processes. This node is the gateway of the physical supply chain.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Purchase Requisition Trigger:** The initial demand signal for raw materials or finished goods.
2. **Freight Consolidation:** Combining multiple small shipments into larger, cost-effective loads (LTL to FTL).
3. **Carrier Selection:** choosing the optimal transportation mode (Air, Sea, Road, Rail) based on dynamic cost/time indices.
4. **Dock Scheduling:** managing the "Appointment Window" to prevent warehouse congestion during peak hours.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Financial Analysis:** calculating the "Total Landed Cost" (Price + Shipping + Duties + Insurance).
*   **Non-Functional Requirements:** defining the accuracy requirements for IoT tracking sensors in cold-chain logistics.
*   **Backlog Management:** prioritizing "Hot Shipments" during inventory shortages.
