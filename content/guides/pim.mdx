# Product Information Mgt (PIM)

Falls under "Manage Product Data" in the APQC framework, serving as the "Single Source of Truth" for catalog data.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Onboarding:** Importing raw product data from manufacturers, ERPs, or spreadsheets.
2. **Enrichment:** Adding SEO-optimized titles, marketing descriptions, and high-res media.
3. **Localization:** Translating content and adjusting units for different regional digital stores.
4. **Syndication:** Pushing the finished product data to Shopify, Amazon, and Print Catalogs.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Data Modelling:** Designing the Attributes (Color, Size, Material) and Taxonomies (Categories).
*   **Glossary:** Defining clear naming conventions to keep the global catalog clean and searchable.
*   **Stakeholder Collaboration:** Reconciling Branding, SEO, and Merchandising requirements.

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Taxonomy is your SEO foundation. As a BA, ensure your categories aren't too deep. A customer should be able to find any product in 3 clicks or less; the PIM structure must reflect that simplicity.
