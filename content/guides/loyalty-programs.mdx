# Loyalty Programs

Part of "Develop and Manage Marketing Programs" (APQC 3.3) in the commerce realm.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Enrolment:** Opt-in capture during checkout or via a dedicated club landing page.
2. **Accrual:** Earning points based on spend thresholds, social follows, or birthdays.
3. **Redemption:** Burning points for discounts, free gifts, or exclusive "Early Access" events.
4. **Churn Management:** Identifying low-activity members for targeted "win-back" campaigns.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Business Case:** Calculating the ROI of points liability versus long-term customer value.
*   **Business Rules Analysis:** Designing expiration, fraud, and stacking rules for rewards.
*   **Decision Analysis:** Choosing between "Tiers" (Bronze/Gold) or "Cash-back" loyalty models.

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Tiered exclusivity is better than points. As a BA, recommend "Early access to new drops" for Gold members over just giving 5% off. High-value customers crave status over small discounts.
