# KYC/KYB & Identity Verification

Directly linked to "Manage Risk and Compliance" and "Customer Onboarding" within financial services frameworks like APQC for Banking.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Data Collection:** Capturing government-issued IDs (passports, licenses) and liveness biometrics.
2. **Document Authentication:** Validating security features on IDs via automated OCR and AI-base liveness checks.
3. **Database Screening:** Checking customer data against PEP (Politically Exposed Persons) and global sanctions lists.
4. **Adverse Media Check:** Periodic or real-time searching for news records indicating financial crime risk.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Non-Functional Requirements:** Defining maximum latency for a "Pass/Fail" decision (e.g., < 30 seconds).
*   **Interface Analysis:** Mapping complex JSON data fields between the mobile app and the KYC API.
*   **Risk Analysis:** Assessing the business impact of "False Positives" on customer drop-off.

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> BAs must design for "Liveness." Static photos are easily faked. Ensure your requirements for the identity flow include active liveness checks (e.g., "turn your head" or "blink") to prevent spoofing attacks.
