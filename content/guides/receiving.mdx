# Receiving Goods

**Receiving** occurs when goods from a supplier arrive at the warehouse. The primary objective is to verify that what was delivered matches what was ordered (in type and quantity).

## Key BA Activities
- **WMS Integration:** Designing interfaces between the Warehouse Management System (WMS) and ERP.
- **Exceptions Handling:** Defining the process when items are damaged or missing (RMA generation).

## Typical Deliverables
- **Use Cases:** Documenting the steps a warehouse clerk takes when a truck arrives (e.g., scanning barcodes, handling discrepancies).
