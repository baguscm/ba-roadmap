# Vendor Management

Aligned with the "Procure Materials and Services" (APQC 4.0) category, Vendor Management focuses on the lifecycle of supplier relationships.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Identification & Selection:** Sourcing vendors that meet quality and cost standards.
2. **Onboarding:** Registering vendors in the ERP and setting up EDI/API connections.
3. **Performance Monitoring:** Using scorecards to track OTIF (On-Time In-Full) metrics.
4. **Contract Renewal/Termination:** Managing the legal and operational end-of-life for supplier relations.

---

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Strategy Analysis:** Defining the sourcing strategy (Single vs Multi-vendor).
*   **Requirements Analysis:** Defining the technical interface requirements for vendor portals.
*   **Solution Evaluation:** Assessing vendor portal performance against business KPIs.

## Typical Deliverables

*   **Vendor Scorecard Template**
*   **Master Service Agreement (MSA) Requirements Document**
*   **API Integration Specification for Vendor Data**

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Your biggest risk in Vendor Management is "Ghost Vendors." As a BA, ensure there are strict validation rules in the onboarding flow to prevent duplicate entries and fraudulent bank details.
