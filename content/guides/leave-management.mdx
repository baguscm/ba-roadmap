# Absence & Leave Management

Found within "Manage Total Rewards" and "Employee Benefit Programs" categories in the APQC hierarchy.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Accrual Logic:** Calculating monthly PTO/Sick accruals based on seniority and employment type.
2. **Request Workflow:** Managing the "Self-Service Request" through to "Manager Decisioning."
3. **Compliance Tracking:** Monitoring and auditing FMLA, Sick Leave, and Statutory leaves for legal audits.
4. **Calendar Integration:** Reflecting approved absences in Outlook/Google and Team calendars.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Business Case:** Analyzing the long-term financial liability of "Unused PTO" on the company's ledger.
*   **Business Rules Analysis:** Precisely defining "Carry-over," "Forfeiture," and "Payout on Termination" rules.
*   **Regulatory Analysis:** Ensuring the system handles state-specific Paid Sick Leave (PSL) requirements.

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Design for "Blackouts." As a BA, ensure your marketing and ops teams can set "Blackout dates" (e.g., Black Friday) where the system won't allow a manager to approve any vacation requests.
