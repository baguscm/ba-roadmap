# Candidate Onboarding

The bridge between external acquisition and the employee lifecycle in the APQC framework.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Employee Self-Service:** High-speed capture of banking, ID, and tax documents via a secure portal.
2. **Compliance Verification:** Processing Background Checks and "Right to Work" (e.g. I-9) legal docs.
3. **Asset & IT Supply:** Automated triggers for laptop ordering and the creation of Slack/Email accounts.
4. **ERP Integration:** Synchronizing the finished candidate record into the Core HRIS and Payroll engine.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Interface Analysis:** Mapping the "Hire" event trigger fields from the ATS (Recruit) to the HRIS (Core).
*   **Data Modelling:** Defining the exact state transition from "Signed Candidate" to "Active Employee."
*   **User Journey Mapping:** Optimizing the first 48 hours of the employee's experience to drive engagement.
