# Supplier Performance

Mappings to "Manage Supplier Relationships" (APQC 4.3.1) and "Monitor Supplier Performance" (APQC 4.3.2). This is where the BA measures the health of the supply chain ecosystem.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Metric Definition:** Establishing the KPIs (Key Performance Indicators) for the vendor network (e.g. Lead Time Accuracy).
2. **Quality Audit:** executing physical and digital checks against the "Agreed Quality Level" (AQL).
3. **Scorecarding:** Aggregating performance data into a monthly or quarterly vendor review.
4. **Development & CAP:** Implementing "Corrective Action Plans" for underperforming suppliers to restore service levels.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Benchmarking:** comparing internal supplier performance against industry peers.
*   **Root Cause Analysis:** identifying why a specific supplier has transitioned from "Green" to "Red" (e.g. Labor shortage, material cost).
*   **Risk Analysis:** calculating the "Concentration Risk" of having too much spend with a single underperforming vendor.

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Don't just punish poor performance. As a BA, design the system to reward "Elite" suppliers (e.g. Early payment terms, first-look at new contracts). Positive reinforcement drives better behavior than purely punitive metrics.
