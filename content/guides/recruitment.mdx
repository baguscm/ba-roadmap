# Recruitment

Found within the "Manage Talent Acquisition" (APQC 7.2) hierarchy of HR processing.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Demand & Budgeting:** Hiring managers requesting new headcount and securing Finance approval.
2. **Multichannel Sourcing:** Distributing job roles to LinkedIn, Indeed, and relevant job boards.
3. **Structured Assessment:** Standardized screening, testing, and interview loops to reduce bias.
4. **Contract Negotiation:** Negotiating base, equity, and benefits, then executing the contract.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Process Analysis:** Mapping the "Candidate Funnel" to find bottleneck stages (e.g. Long technical tests).
*   **Stakeholder Collaboration:** Reconciling the Manager's sense of urgency with HR's compliance standards.
*   **Non-Functional Requirements:** Designing for peak load during company-wide "Hiring Sprints."

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Source of Hire is your ROI. As a BA, ensure your ATS tracks exactly where your top 10% of performers came from. If LinkedIn provides 90% of hires but Employee Referrals provide 90% of "Top Performers," pivot your budget.
