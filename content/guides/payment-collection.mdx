# Payment Collection

Managing the "Order to Cash" finale, specifically "Process Customer Payments" in the APQC framework.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Authorization:** Reserving funds on the customer's card at the time of order placement.
2. **Capture:** Actually withdrawing the funds once the warehouse confirms the item has shipped.
3. **Settlement:** The payment gateway moving the funds into the merchant's bank account (T+2/T+3).
4. **Refund/Chargeback:** Handling the reversal of transactions and defending against fraud claims.

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Don't "Capture" until you ship. Legally and operationally, it's best practice in e-commerce to only authorize funds at checkout and only execute the capture once the warehouse confirms the box is on the truck.
