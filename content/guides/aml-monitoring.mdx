# AML Monitoring

Covers "Ensure Financial Compliance" and "Enterprise Risk Management" (APQC 11.2).

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Rule Logic Execution:** Running "Structuring" and "Velocity" checks on every single platform transaction.
2. **Alert Management:** Flagging behavior that deviates from a customer's historic "Normal" behavior.
3. **Case Investigation:** A compliance analyst reviewing the flagged alert and collecting KYC evidence.
4. **Regulatory Submission:** Formal filing of SARs (Suspicious Activity Reports) to government entities (e.g. FinCEN).

---

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Root Cause Analysis:** Investigating the behavioral source of "False Positive" rule triggers.
*   **Prototyping:** Designing the Case Management dashboard for compliance operations.
*   **Data Modelling:** Designing the "Entity Resolution" logic to link different accounts to the same person.

## Typical Deliverables

*   **Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Alert Rule Set**
*   **Case Management Workflow (BPMN)**
*   **Compliance Analyst Performance Dashboard**

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Rules should be dynamic. As a BA, don't just design "Fixed Thresholds" (e.g., > $10,000). Design "Relative Thresholds" (e.g., > 300% of average monthly volume) to catch smarter laundering.
