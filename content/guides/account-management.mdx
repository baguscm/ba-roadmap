# Account Management

Relates to "Perform Financial Transactions" and "Manage Customer Service" in the banking APQC hierarchy.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Account Provisioning:** Defining the default ledger state and sub-account structure upon approval.
2. **Maintenance:** Handling changes to transaction limits, profile details, or linked bank accounts.
3. **State Transitions:** Managing accounts being placed on "Legal Hold" or "Administrative Lock."
4. **Closure:** Handling final interest accruals, fee sweeps, and fund disbursement during closure.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **State Modelling:** Defining the exhaustive list of account statuses and all possible transitions.
*   **Organization Modelling:** Establishing security profiling for "Admin Only" account modifications.
*   **Process Modeling:** Mapping the complex flow for re-opening or transferring previously closed accounts.

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
