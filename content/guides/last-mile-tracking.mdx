# Last Mile Tracking

The intersection of "Manage Outbound Transportation" (APQC 6.2) and "Execute Customer Orders" (APQC 5.0). This is the final and most visible link in the consumer supply chain.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Out-for-Delivery Trigger:** The moment the parcel is scanned into a regional delivery van or courier vehicle.
2. **Proximity Geofencing:** triggering automated customer notifications (SMS/Email) when the driver is within X kilometers of the destination.
3. **Delivery Attempt:** Executing the physical drop-off, which may include signature capture or "Safe Place" photo evidence.
4. **POD Capture:** Digitally archival of the "Proof of Delivery" (POD) to close the financial loop and prevent "Where-Is-My-Order" (WISMO) disputes.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Prototyping:** designing a mobile-friendly tracking page that accurately manages customer expectations during peak delays.
*   **Data Analysis:** analyzing "Failed Delivery Attempt" data to optimize courier routing and customer communication windows.
*   **Risk Analysis:** managing the liability of high-value deliveries that require authenticated signatures vs. photo-drop Evidence.
