# Logistics & Ore Shipping

Associated with "Manage Logistics" (APQC 6.0) and "Execute Customer Orders" (APQC 5.0). This is the critical outbound link from the mine-gate to the global market.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Stockpile Orchestration:** managing the "Age" and "Quality" of ore in the stockpile to meet specific blend requirements.
2. **Rail & Road Freight:** Orchestrating the massive movement of bulk commodities from the mine-site to the port or railhead.
3. **Product Blending:** mixing different ore grades to meet the high-accuracy purity and chemical specifications demanded by the buyer.
4. **Vessel Scheduling:** managing the arrival of massive bulk-carrier ships and the high-speed loading process to avoid "Demurrage" (late-fees).

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Interface Analysis:** mapping the flow of "Purity Samples" from the lab system to the commercial invoice.
*   **Business Rules Analysis:** defining the "Thresholds" for rejecting a shipment based on high moisture or impurity levels.
*   **Data Modelling:** tracking the "Movements" of ore across Stockpiles, Trains, and Ships to maintain an accurate inventory count.

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Grade is King. As a BA, your most important logic is the "Blending Optimizer." If you ship ore that is too high-grade, you are "Giving away" value for free. If it's too low-grade, you face massive financial penalties. Precision in the blending data model is what drives mining profitability.
