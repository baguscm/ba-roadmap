# Benefits Administration

Directly maps to the "Manage Total Rewards" and "Manage Employee Benefit Programs" (APQC 7.3) segments.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Plan Configuration:** Building health, retirement, and insurance plan options within the HRIS.
2. **Eligibility Logic:** Defining who is entitled to what based on tenure, location, and role.
3. **Open Enrolment:** Orchestrating the annual period where employees make their benefit elections.
4. **Carrier Data Exchange:** Transmitting enrollment (EDI 834) files to external providers to activate coverage.

---

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Business Rules Analysis:** Mapping complex eligibility, premium-splitting, and coverage rules.
*   **Interface Analysis:** Defining and auditing the EDI protocols for different insurance carriers.
*   **Vendor Assessment:** Evaluating the usability and accessibility of the benefits enrollment experience.

## Typical Deliverables

*   **Employee-Benefit Eligibility Matrix**
*   **Carrier Data Feed (EDI) Specification**
*   **Annual Enrollment Lifecycle Map**

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> EDI is where it breaks. As a BA, be incredibly precise with your data formats. A single mismatched character in a health insurance feed can lead to an employee being denied coverage at the hospital.
