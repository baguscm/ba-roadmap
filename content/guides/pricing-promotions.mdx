# Pricing & Promotions

A key part of "Manage Marketing and Sales" (APQC 3.0), focusing on the execution of price strategy.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Price Book Management:** Setting base and regional prices for global storefronts.
2. **Promotion Engine:** Configuring "BOGO" (Buy One Get One) or "Percent-Off" logic.
3. **Coupon Management:** Generating, distributing, and tracking the usage of discount codes.
4. **Discount Stacking Rules:** Defining logic for how multiple discounts interact on a single cart.

---

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Business Rules Analysis:** Defining complex "Stacking" and "Exclusion" rules for codes.
*   **Decision Modelling:** Determining the final price after tax, shipping, and multiple discounts.
*   **Risk Analysis:** Assessing the "Profitability Floor" to prevent accidental 100% discounts.

## Typical Deliverables

*   **Promotion Logic Matrix**
*   **Stacked Discount Rule Set**
*   **Coupon Management API Specification**

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Stacking is the profit killer. As a BA, always design your promotion engine with a "Master Exclusive" flag, so that a 50% clearance item can skip the "10% Welcome" coupon.
