# Offboarding & Exit Processes

Part of "Manage Employee Information" and "Perform Employee Separation" (APQC 7.4.3).

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Separation Trigger:** Recording the resignation, retirement, or involuntary notice in the HRIS.
2. **Asset Recovery:** Tracking the physical return of company hardware (Laptops, ID badges, Corporate Cards).
3. **Exit Interview:** Gathering data via self-service or interview to understand attrition root-causes.
4. **Access Revocation:** The automated de-provisioning of system access within minutes of termination.
