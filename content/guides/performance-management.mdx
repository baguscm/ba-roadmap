# Performance Management

A key component of "Manage Talent Performance" (APQC 7.5) for the enterprise workforce.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Goal Cascading:** Breaking down corporate OKRs into measurable, individual-contributor KPIs.
2. **Ongoing Check-ins:** Documenting monthly 1-on-1s between managers and reports for an audit trail.
3. **Calibration:** Reviewing performance ratings across departments to ensure fairness and consistency.
4. **Succession Planning:** Identifying "High-Potential" (HiPo) employees for future leadership training.

---

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Organization Modelling:** Designing the cross-functional "Calibration" groups for leadership reviews.
*   **Prototyping:** Designing intuitive, "low-friction" feedback forms to encourage regular, honest input.
*   **Metric Calibration:** Ensuring that a "Top Performer" rating means the same thing in Sales as it does in IT.

*   **Annual Performance Review Workflow**

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Avoid "Recency Bias." As a BA, design the system to capture mini-notes throughout the year. If you only look at November's performance for a December review, you'll miss the great work done in March.
