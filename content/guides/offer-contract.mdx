# Offer & Contract

**Offer & Contract** is the closing stage of the **Recruitment** funnel. It involves the formal documentation of the compensation and benefits package, the generation of a legally-compliant employment contract, and the final negotiation with the candidate.

## Node Knowledge: The Closing Stage
1. **Offer Approval:** Routing the final package (Base Salary, Bonus, Equity, Benefits) for internal sign-off.
2. **Contract Generation:** Using legally-approved templates to create the official offer letter or contract.
3. **Electronic Signature:** Distributing the documents for secure, legally binding signing (e.g., DocuSign).
4. **Negotiation Management:** Tracking "Counter-offers" and managing amendments to the contract terms.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

*   **Financial Analysis:** ensure the final offer stays within the "Requisition Budget" approved in Phase 1.
*   **Requirements Validation:** ensuring that the "Start Date" and "Legal Entity" on the contract match the downstream payroll and onboarding system requirements.

## Typical Deliverables

*   **Contract Template Matrix:** A guide for HR to select the correct legal document based on the hire's location and role.
*   **Compensation Ben-Fit Analysis:** A comparison tool used during negotiation to prove value.

## 💡 Expert BA Review

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Timing kills offers. If a candidate says "Yes" on a Friday, but your internal approval takes until next Wednesday, you might lose them. As a BA, you should automate the "Contract Generation" so that as soon as a recruiter gets a verbal "Yes" and clicks a button, the system generates and routes the contract for signing in minutes, not days.
