# Customer Order Management

The core of "Execute Customer Orders" (APQC 5.0). It covers the transition from browsing to commitment.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Order Capture:** Synchronizing the website basket with the back-end OMS.
2. **Credit/Fraud Check:** Triggering risk scores (e.g., via Signifyd or Kount).
3. **Inventory Commitment:** Hard-allocating stock so it can't be sold twice.
4. **Order Orchestration:** Routing the order to the nearest or cheapest warehouse.

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> The "Pending Payment" state is where orders go to die. Ensure you have automated triggers to notify a customer if their credit card is declined, allowing them to fix it without losing their cart.
