# Customer Helpdesk

Aligned with "Manage Customer Service" (APQC 5.4), closing the post-purchase customer loop.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Ticket Creation:** Multichannel capture from email, chat, phone, or Social DM.
2. **Routing:** Automatically assigning to the specialist agent (e.g. "Returns Specialist").
3. **Resolution:** Providing the fix, issuing a manual discount, or approving an RMA.
4. **Satisfaction Survey (CSAT):** Closing the loop by measuring the customer's sentiment.

---

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Process Modeling:** Mapping the "Escalation to Management" and "RMA Approval" workflows.
*   **Non-Functional Requirements:** Defining strict response times (SLAs) for VIP customer tiers.
*   **Requirements Management:** Synthesizing common complaints into future product feature requests.

## Typical Deliverables

*   **Support Ticket Status Workflow Diagram**
*   **SLA Definition Document (Priority Matrix)**
*   **Auto-Response Template Requirements**

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Macro power! As a BA, focus on designing "Macros" (pre-written responses). If 60% of your tickets are "Where is my order?", automate a response that pulls live tracking status from the WMS.
