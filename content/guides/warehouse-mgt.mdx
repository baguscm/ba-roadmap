# Warehouse Management (WMS)

Associated with "Manage Logistics and Warehousing" (APQC 6.4). The orchestrator of physical inventory movement and storage optimization.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Receiving & Put-away:** Validating inbound goods and directing them to the optimal storage bin based on volume/velocity.
2. **Inventory Management:** Maintaining the integrity of the "Digital Twin" of the physical warehouse stock.
3. **Picking & Packing:** Executing the selection of items for outbound orders using various methodologies (Wave, Cluster, Zone).
4. **Labor Management:** Tracking and optimizing the productivity of the workforce across travel time and tasks.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Observation:** conducting "Day-in-the-Life" (DILO) studies of warehouse pickers to identify UI bottlenecks.
*   **Non-Functional Requirements:** defining the Wi-Fi/Radio-Frequency coverage requirements for mobile scanners in high-density rack areas.
*   **Data Modelling:** mapping the complex relationships between Items, Bins, Lots, and Unit-of-Measure (UOM).

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Travel time is your biggest cost. As a BA, focus your efforts on "Slotting Optimization." If a picker has to walk 100 meters to get the most popular item in the warehouse, the system has failed. Popularity changes; your Bin mapping should too.
