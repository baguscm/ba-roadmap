# Employee Data Management

The central core of "Manage Employee Information" (APQC 7.4) in the business enterprise.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Direct Integration:** Capturing the clean, validated new hire record from onboarding.
2. **Global Self-Service:** Giving employees the ability to update addresses, bank details, or next-of-kin.
3. **Core Maintenance:** Tracking internal movements, job changes, and management hierarchies.
4. **Data Stewardship:** Maintaining the "Effective Date" for every single change to the employee record.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Data Modelling:** Designing the "Global Master Employee Record" schema across different physical locations.
*   **Business Rules Analysis:** Defining "Self-service" vs "Admin-only" field editing permissions (e.g. Can't change your own salary).
*   **Risk Analysis (GDPR):** Ensuring "Personally Identifiable Information" (PII) is encrypted and auditable.

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Global consistency is hard. As a BA, if you are designing for a multinational, ensure the system can handle different address formats, character sets (e.g., Cyrillic), and local ID numbers without breaking your reporting.
