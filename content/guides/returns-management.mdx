# Returns Management

Aligned with "Manage Product Returns" (APQC 6.5), often referred to as Reverse Logistics.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **RMA Request:** The customer initiating a Return Merchandise Authorization via a self-service portal.
2. **Reverse Logistics:** Generating a return shipping label and tracking the incoming package.
3. **Disposition:** Inspecting the item to decide if it is "Restockable," "Repairable," or "Damaged."
4. **Refund/Store Credit:** Closing the loop with the customer once the item is physically validated.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Sequence Diagrams:** Tracing the credit note through the financial ledger to ensure tax reversals.
*   **Process Modeling:** Mapping the "Return to Store" vs "Return to Warehouse" omnichannel flows.
*   **Survey/Questionnaire:** Eliciting the top reasons for returns to drive product improvement.
