# Outbound & Distribution

Mapped to "Manage Outbound Transportation" (APQC 6.2) and "Manage Inbound Logistics" (APQC 6.2.1) in the Global Supply Chain forum. This node governs the journey from the dock to the customer.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Load Optimization:** Consolidating outbound orders to maximize truck capacity (FTL) and minimize carbon footprint.
2. **Carrier Selection (TMS):** Using a "Least Cost / Most Performance" algorithm to assign shipments to specific carriers.
3. **Cross-docking:** Moving items directly from receiving to outbound shipping without intermediate storage.
4. **Freight Audit:** Ensuring the bill received from the carrier matches the agreed contractual rates exactly.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Strategy Analysis:** determining the cost/benefit of "Same Day" delivery vs. standard shipping tiers.
*   **Business Rules Analysis:** mapping the complex logic of "Dimensional Weight" across 15+ different global carriers.
*   **Stakeholder Collaboration:** reconciling Finance's "Budget Caps" with Sales' "Fastest Shipping" promises.
