# Picking & Packing

Mapped to "Manage Logistics and Warehousing" (APQC 6.4) and specifically "Pick, Pack, and Ship" (APQC 6.4.3). This is the high-velocity execution layer of fulfillment.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Wave Management:** Releasing orders in batches (waves) to the floor to maximize picking efficiency.
2. **Directed Picking:** Navigating the warehouse via mobile instructions using methodologies like Zone, Cluster, or Discrete picking.
3. **Quality Validation:** Scanning items at the packing station to ensure 100% accuracy before the box is taped.
4. **Manifesting:** Generating shipping labels and carrier documentation (Bol, Manifest) in real-time.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Process Analysis:** identifying bottlenecks at the packing station (e.g. searching for the right box size).
*   **Interface Analysis:** mapping scanner data (GS1-128 barcodes) to the WMS inventory decrement logic.
*   **Prototyping:** designing intuitive "Hands-free" picking interfaces (Voice-to-pick or AR glasses).

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Don't forget about "Box Suitability." As a BA, ensure your system suggests the smallest possible box for every order. If you ship a USB stick in a laptop box, you are paying for air and destroying your outbound margins.
