# Invoicing & Payment

The final step in **Procure to Pay (P2P)**. This is where Accounts Payable matches the Purchase Order, the Receiving document (Goods Receipt), and the Vendor's Invoice. If they match (3-way match), payment is released.

## Key BA Activities
- **3-Way Match Rules:** Defining exactly how strict the matching is (e.g., allow a 2% variance on total cost).
- **Payment Gateways:** Gathering requirements for integrating automated clearing house (ACH) or wire transfer systems.

## Typical Deliverables
- **State Machine Diagram:** Showing the lifecycle of an Invoice (Received -> Pending Match -> Approved -> Paid).
