# Account Reconciliation

A key part of "Manage Financial Resources," closing the book on the daily sales activity.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Statement Retrieval:** Pulling daily reports from bank accounts and gateways (Stripe/PayPal/Adyen).
2. **Matching Logic:** Linking one or many bank deposits to individual internal Sales Orders.
3. **Variance Management:** Determining why the bank received $97 but the order was $100 (Gateway Fees).
4. **Adjusting Entries:** Automatically booking fees to the correct GL expense ledger.

---

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Gateway fees are the hidden variance. As a BA, ensure your system captures "Gross proceeds" and "Gateway Fee" separately at the point of reconciliation, otherwise your net margin will always be wrong.
