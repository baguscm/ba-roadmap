# Extraction Operations

Mapped to "Execute Operations" (APQC 4.0) and "Produce Product" (APQC 6.0) in the mining lifecycle. This is the physical execution of the mine plan.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Drill & Blast:** Executing the fragmentation of the rock mass to enable efficient loading.
2. **Load & Haul:** Orchestrating the fleet of excavators and massive haul trucks to move ore to the crusher and waste to the dump.
3. **Crushing & Processing:** The initial physical transformation of the ore into a transportable or tradable product.
4. **Fleet Analytics:** monitoring real-time diagnostics of the heavy equipment fleet to maximize availability and minimize fuel burn.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Process Analysis:** Identifying bottle-necks in the hauling cycle (e.g. truck queuing at the excavator).
*   **Non-Functional Requirements:** defining the performance requirements for the Private LTE/Mesh networks required for autonomous haulage systems.
*   **Data Analysis:** analyzing "Payload Variance" to ensure trucks are consistently loaded to their safe capacity.
