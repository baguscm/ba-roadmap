# Time Tracking

A critical part of "Manage Time and Attendance" (APQC 7.4.2) for payroll accuracy.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Multi-device Capture:** Recording punches or duration via Mobile, Web, or Physical biometric clocks.
2. **Rule Evaluation:** Applying complex logic for Overtime (OT), Shift Premiums, and Rounding rules.
3. **Approval Flow:** Providing an "Exception-based" review for managers to sign-off on timecards.
4. **Payroll Integration:** Exporting the "Evaluated Payable Time" into the Payroll engine for calculation.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Process Modeling:** Mapping the complex "California Overtime" laws (and other regional rules) into logic steps.
*   **Non-Functional Requirements:** Evaluating the ruggedness and offline capabilities of time-clock hardware.
*   **Data Analysis:** Using time data to track "Absenteeism" and "Labor Leakage" trends for the Ops team.
