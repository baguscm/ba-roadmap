# Payment Gateways

Focuses on the "Process Customer Payments" segment of the O2C cycle in a FinTech context.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Authentication:** 3D Secure and PSD2-compliant multi-factor authentication checks.
2. **Authorization:** Requesting the "Auth" from the issuing bank via the card networks.
3. **Capture:** The final execution signal to move the physical money after authorization.
4. **Notification:** Managing real-time webhooks (success/failure) for merchants.

---

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Sequence Diagrams:** Mapping the sub-second communication between Website, Gateway, and Processor.
*   **Risk Analysis:** Tracking and mitigating "Chargeback Ratios" and fraud level triggers.
*   **Interface Analysis:** Mapping raw technical gateway responses into clear, localized user messages.

## Typical Deliverables

*   **Payment Webhook Data Map**
*   **Checkout / Modal UI Requirements**
*   **Payment Retry & Fallback Strategy**

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Don't ignore the "Retry" logic. As a BA, define how many times a system should attempt a "Soft Decline" (network timeout) before failing the transaction.
