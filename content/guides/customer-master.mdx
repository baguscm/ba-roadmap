# Customer Master Data

In the **Order to Cash (O2C)** cycle, the **Customer Master** is the foundational "Golden Record" that enables all downstream activities. Without accurate customer data, billing fails, shipping is delayed, and credit risk cannot be managed.

## Node Knowledge: The Data Lifecycle
1. **Onboarding:** Collecting legal entity names, tax IDs (VAT/GST), and contact info.
2. **Standardization:** Ensuring addresses follow global standards (e.g., ISO) for freight accuracy.
3. **Internal Hierarchy:** Mapping the "Bill-to", "Ship-to", and "Payer" relationship.
4. **Maintenance:** Updating bank details and credit status periodically.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

*   **Requirement Analysis:** Defining the mandatory fields required by the ERP vs. the CRM.
*   **Data Modelling:** Documenting the entity relationships (Account vs. Contact vs. Address).
*   **Process Analysis:** Identifying bottlenecks in the "New Customer Setup" workflow that delay the first Sales Order.

## Typical Deliverables

*   **Data Dictionary:** A detailed list of all customer fields, their data types, and validation logic.
*   **Data Migration Plan:** Strategy for cleaning and moving customer records from legacy systems to the new environment.

## 💡 Expert BA Review

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Don't underestimate the complexity of "Customer Hierarchies". A customer might be a small branch office, but they are owned by a global parent. You must define early in the project *who* receives the invoice and *where* the goods are physically delivered. Getting the Bill-To/Ship-To relationship wrong is the #1 cause of payment disputes.
