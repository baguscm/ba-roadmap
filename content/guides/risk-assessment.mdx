# Risk Assessment & Profiling

A subset of "Evaluate and Manage Risk" (APQC 11.0), specifically tailored for financial exposures and credit risk.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Data Enrichment:** Pulling bureau scores (FICO, TransUnion) and historical bank transaction data.
2. **Scoring Engine:** Applying weighted business rules to determine a consolidated risk score.
3. **Risk Classification:** Automating the categorization of a user as Low, Medium, or High risk.
4. **Ongoing Monitoring:** Re-evaluating the risk profile based on changing spend patterns or credit signals.

---

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Decision Modelling (DMN):** Creating formal notation for complex credit and risk approval logic.
*   **Business Rules Analysis:** Documenting the "Knock-out" criteria (e.g., debt-to-income > 40%).
*   **Data Analysis:** Identifying correlations between initial risk classifications and actual default rates.

## Typical Deliverables

*   **Decision Table (Risk Matrix)**
*   **Credit Score Integration Specification**
*   **Risk Threshold Rule Set**
