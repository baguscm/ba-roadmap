# Loan Origination

Directly maps to "Manage Lending" category in the BFS APQC framework (APQC 7.0 for Banking).

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Pre-Qualification:** Rapid assessment of a borrower's base eligibility using a "Soft Pull."
2. **Full Underwriting:** Comprehensive analysis of financial evidence, employment, and income verification.
3. **Closing & Disclosure:** Generating, sending, and digitally signing "Truth in Lending" (TILA) compliance docs.
4. **Funding:** Wire disbursement of the approved loan amount to the borrower's designated bank account.
