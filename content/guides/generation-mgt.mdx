# Energy Generation Mgt

A foundational node in the Energy sector, focusing on the production of power and "Managing Operations" within the Utility framework.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Capacity Planning:** Forecasting long-term energy demand and scheduling generation assets to meet the base load.
2. **Operations & Dispatch:** managing the real-time firing of assets (Coal, Gas, Nuclear, Hydro) to balance the grid.
3. **Outage Management:** Scheduling preventative maintenance and managing forced outages to minimize downtime.
4. **HSSE Monitoring:** Ensuring strict adherence to Health, Safety, Security, and Environmental (HSSE) regulations required for energy production.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Risk Analysis:** modeling the impact of a plant failure on regional grid stability.
*   **Business Rules Analysis:** defining the "Thresholds" for ramping up expensive peaker plants during peak demand.
*   **Data Modelling:** mapping the high-frequency telemetry data from SCADA systems into actionable business reports.
