# HSSE & Safety Compliance

The legal and ethical bedrock of mining, mapped to "Manage External Relationships" (APQC 11.0) and "Manage Health, Safety, and Environment" (APQC 11.1).

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Risk Management:** systematically identifying "Fatal Risks" (e.g. Roof-falls, Traffic-interactions) and implementing critical controls.
2. **Incident Investigation:** Orchestrating the "Root Cause Analysis" (RCA) and documenting corrective actions after a safety event.
3. **Fatigue Monitoring:** Using wearable tech and biometric data to ensure heavy equipment operators are fit-for-duty.
4. **Environmental Stewardship:** monitoring air quality, water discharge, and land rehabilitation progress against statutory licenses.

## Key BA Activities (BABOK® Alignment)

In alignment with the *Business Analysis Body of Knowledge (BABOK®)*, mastering this node relies on the following knowledge areas and techniques:

*   **Document Analysis:** verifying that the "Standard Operating Procedures" (SOPs) meet the latest MSHA/OSHA safety standards.
*   **Root Cause Analysis:** Using the "5-Whys" or "Fishbone" techniques to identify systemic safety gaps.
*   **Stakeholder Collaboration:** Facilitating workshops between the Safety team and Operations to ensure safety is "Built-in," not "Bolted-on."

## 💡 Expert BA Review & Adjustments

> [!TIP]
> **From the Desk of a Lead Business Analyst**
> Culture is the best "Solution." As a BA, don't just build a reporting system. Design your safety portal so it is easy to report "Near-misses." If it takes 20 minutes to fill out a form, people won't do it. If it takes 20 seconds, you get the data you need to prevent the next fatality.
