# Gross-to-Net Calculations

The calculation engine at the heart of "Process Payroll" (APQC 9.3) in financial services.

## Node Knowledge: The Process Lifecycle
1. **Gross Accumulation:** Summing Base Pay, Overtime, Commissions, and specialized Bonuses.
2. **Pre-Tax Deductions:** Subtracting 401(k) contributions, Medical Premiums, and FSA/HSA funds.
3. **Statutory Taxing:** Applying the logic for Federal, State, and local (SUI/SIT/FICA) withholdings.
4. **Disbursement:** The automated move of the final "Net Pay" into the employee's bank account via ACH.
